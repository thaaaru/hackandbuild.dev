Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior experience?

No! Our beginner courses start from the basics. We'll teach you everything you need to know, step by step.

What if I miss a session?

All sessions are recorded. You can catch up at your own pace and ask questions in our community forum.

Are there Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions?

CTFs are optional but encouraged! We run monthly challenges to practice your skills in a fun, competitive environment.

Can I get a certificate?

Yes! Complete any course and you'll receive a certificate. Perfect for your LinkedIn profile and job applications.
